Dr. Li Ming is a postdoctoral researcher at NJU AeroSafe Laboratory. He completed his PhD in Aerospace Engineering at Nanjing University, where his dissertation focused on "Deep Learning-Based Fault Diagnosis for Aircraft Systems." His current research involves developing advanced machine learning models for real-time health monitoring of aircraft components.

Dr. Li has published over 20 papers in top-tier journals and conferences. He is actively involved in several industry collaboration projects, working with aviation companies to implement AI-driven maintenance solutions. He also mentors graduate students and contributes to the lab's software development initiatives.
